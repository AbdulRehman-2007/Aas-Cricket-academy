About AAS Cricket Academy

At AAS Cricket Academy, we are dedicated to delivering one of the best cricket training experiences in the region. Under the guidance of our highly experienced Head Coach, Saleem Haider, and supported by two skilled assistant coaches, we ensure that every player receives the attention and mentorship they deserve.

Our academy offers five days of structured practice sessions every week across two different venues, providing variety and exposure to different playing conditions. We pride ourselves on having well-maintained nets and top-quality training facilities, designed to help players sharpen their skills and improve their game.

For those seeking personalized growth, we also provide one-to-one coaching sessions directly with Head Coach Saleem Haider, allowing players to focus on specific areas of development. To add a competitive edge, the academy organizes one match every week, giving players the opportunity to apply their training in real game situations.

At AAS Cricket Academy, we don’t just train cricketers—we nurture talent, build discipline, and create opportunities for players to shine.
